The Challenge

Help Steel Warriors tackle knife crime through design

Steel Warriors is a London-based charity that provides gyms to transform the lives of young people affected by crime, violence and social exclusion, giving them the skills and confidence to succeed in life. Spectacularly, the gyms that Steel Warriors provides are made using steel collected by melting down confiscated knives.

Steel Warriors needed another round of funding from their investors, to progress with their ongoing initiatives to tackle knife crime. One of the KPIs they needed to achieve to get funding was by tracking data and activity of people using their gyms. Steel Warriors wanted to do this in way which enriches and enhances the experience of using their facilities. Steel Warriors approached thoughtbot with an ambitious plan of developing a digital platform to support their physical offering. At the outset we discussed a series of broad features:

  • Fitness Tracking
  • Workout Instruction
  • Community Building

Once we downloaded all of the information about their business goals and needs we set to work with a research phase.
