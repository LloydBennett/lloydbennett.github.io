A Re-Design For The Modern Web

Leicestershire based Media Design agency and training provider, Seed Creativity ran a module my university programme called “Creative Media Entrepreneurship”. As part of the module I was required to choose an existing employer and pitch a product idea.Having undertaken research on several digital enterprises in the Leicestershire area, I noticed that Seed Creativity themselves, lacked an engaging and rich web experience that met the needs of the modern consumer. Seed Creativity required a re-design that was malleable and communicated the vision of the brand effectively. I pitched this idea to company directors, which they loved. The plans for the re-design shortly commenced.

01

Who Are We Designing For?

In order to carry out a successful website re-design, it was important we understood who were targeting, why they may visit the site and what they may be looking for.This information was learnt in discovery phase through a business needs analysis with the company directors. This then led to getting into dialogue with their clients and carrying out user tests (surveys, usability tests).

02

Wireframming

Using the information gathered from the discovery phase enabled me to a create visual blueprint that discerned how content will be displayed and the user’s journey on the site.
