A Re-Design For The Modern Web

Leicestershire based Media Design agency and training provider, Seed Creativity ran a module in my university programme called “Creative Media Entrepreneurship”. As part of the module I was required to choose an existing company and pitch a product idea. Having undertaken research on several digital enterprises in the Leicestershire area, I noticed that Seed Creativity themselves, lacked an engaging and rich web experience that met the needs of the modern consumer. Seed Creativity required a re-design that was responsive and communicated the vision of the brand effectively. I pitched this idea to the company directors, which they loved. The plans for the re-design shortly commenced.

01

Who Are We Designing For?

In order to carry out a successful website re-design, it was important that myself and the company directors fully understood who we were targeting, their reasons visiting the site and what they may be looking for. This information was learnt in discovery phase through a business needs analysis with the company directors. This then led to me getting into dialogue with Seed's clients and carrying out user tests (surveys, usability tests).

02

Wireframming

The information gathered from the discovery phase enabled me to a create visual blueprint which discerned how content will be laid out to aid the user’s journey on the site. The wireframes were designed with mobile-first in mind, enabling the company directors and I to be really critical over our content by cutting out the fat.

Featured Video

The homepage has two main call-to-actions: "Watch Video" which play's the featured video and "Learn More" that the user to the feature content page.

A Mobile Menu which pushes the body content out of view when opened.

03

Visual Design

Everything from colour swatches, typographic style rules, layout modules and components were created in a style guide. The style guide provided me with a foundation to compose full page mock-ups.

Big Digital Switchover

A marketing consultation occupied the masthead on the Home Page as this is at the forefront of their sales pitch to win new customers.

Video Testimonals

An effective and convincing way for previous clients to really endorse Seed's services to a potiental new consumer.
