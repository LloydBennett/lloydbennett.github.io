Skillset & Tools of the trade

Web Design & Development, App Design, Rapid Prototyping, Back-end

My first love for the web emerged sometime in the summer of 2013 after my first year of University. I realised the endless possibilities with HTML5 & CSS3 in creating beautiful and engaging websites. Since then, I’ve been plugging away at mock-ups in Photoshop / Sketch through to front-end code, striving to create the best possible user experiences. In my spare time I like to keep up to date with the latest trends and news in design. A Chrome Plugin called Panda, makes this experience all the more enjoyable as it aggregates all the latest articles and posts from my favourite websites such as Awwwards, HackingUI, Medium and Dribbble in one tab. I am currently available to take on projects, so feel free to contact me regarding any work or enquires.
